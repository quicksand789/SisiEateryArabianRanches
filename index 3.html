Our Story

Inspired by the glamorous yet untamed, edgy yet loving, Empress Sisi.

Inspired by the charming soul of Vienna & by the spirit of the legendary Empress Sisi, Sisi's Eatery is a cultural culinary journey made to whomever dares to explore. Austria's capital is endearingly charming, known for its streets paved of culture, history, music and art. From there, came the idea of bringing an Austrian flair to the world.
